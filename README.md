# GigCredit AI

GigCredit AI is an AI-powered micro-loan eligibility platform designed specifically for gig economy workers such as Swiggy delivery partners, Uber drivers, Rapido riders, and freelancers.

Traditional banking systems often reject gig workers because they lack fixed salaries or salary slips. GigCredit AI solves this problem by analyzing alternative financial signals such as digital earnings, transaction history, and platform activity to determine real creditworthiness.

The platform provides eligibility analysis, financial simulations, loan recommendations, and smart financial tools to help gig workers access credit more easily.

---

## Problem Statement

Millions of gig workers are excluded from traditional financial systems because they do not have:

- Salary slips
- Formal employment records
- Stable monthly income

As a result, banks often reject their loan applications even if they have consistent digital earnings.

GigCredit AI addresses this gap by using AI-driven analysis of digital financial data to estimate a worker's real financial reliability.

---

## Key Features

### Loan Eligibility Analyzer
Users can upload financial documents such as:

- Bank statements
- Platform earnings reports
- Vehicle ownership proof

The system evaluates these documents and generates a **trustworthiness score from 0–100**, indicating the user's loan eligibility.

---

### Score History Timeline
The platform tracks eligibility scores across multiple sessions so users can monitor improvements in their financial reliability over time.

---

### Loan Eligibility Simulator
Users can simulate their financial situation by adjusting variables like:

- Monthly income
- Expenses
- EMI obligations
- Digital transaction activity

The system then predicts a reliability score based on these inputs.

---

### Loan Product Matcher
Based on the eligibility score, the system recommends suitable loan products such as:

- MUDRA Micro Loans
- KreditBee Instant Loans
- MoneyTap Credit Lines

Users can directly navigate to the provider's application page.

---

### Smart Financial Tools

The platform also includes tools to help users improve financial readiness:

**Document Readiness Checklist**
Ensures users have all required documents before applying for loans.

**Fraud Detection**
Analyzes financial patterns to detect suspicious activities.

**Expense Tracker**
Helps users track monthly spending and improve financial habits.

**Collateral Estimator**
Estimates potential loan amounts based on asset ownership.

---

### AI Loan Assistant
An AI-powered chatbot helps users with questions related to:

- Loan eligibility
- EMI calculations
- Credit score improvement
- Financial planning

The assistant supports both text and voice interaction.
